About LifeWeb

LifeWeb is a partnership platform that strengthens financing for conservation action to conserve biodiversity, secure livelihoods and address climate change, through implementation of the Strategic Plan for Biodiversity 2011-2020 and the CBD Programme of Work on Protected Areas.
